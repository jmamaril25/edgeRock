Grand Millennium Center

US-31 and Park Street, Westfield, IN 46074

More pictures

More Information

The Millennium Center is the name of the multi use project that will include the following proposed uses. The Westfield Convention and Meeting Center combines Westfield Washington Schools, City of Westfield Government Services, Public Library, Washington Township Trustee office, Business incubator, and other community meeting spaces in one 150,000 square foot, state of the art, convention center. Up to 450 Key Marriott "Delta" full service hotel combined with a limited services hotel developed by Joe Schwars, owner operator of the 400 key Marriott located on East Side of Indy at I 70 and 465. YMCA conjoined with a health services provider similar to new downtown Indianapolis YMCA, with an Olympic size swimming and diving facility to be used by YMCA, Schools, and community. Professional Office Buildings (POB) such as KAR Auction Services. High density / high quality housing (Condos, town homes, apartments). Multiple parking structures as needed for POB, City Building, Community parking for Grand Junction. Many amenities surround the area, most within walking the trail system (see aerial)

  1. 20 plus restaurants
  2. 250 new Hotel rooms opening Summer and Fall of 2017
  3. Grand Park Sports Park
  4. Grand Junction Downtown Westfield
  5. New Riverview Hospital at US 31 and SR 32.
  6. New Dental Hospital at US 31 and SR 32
  7. Several existing and new apartment complex within walking distance
  8. Many single family subdivisions with 10 minute drive
